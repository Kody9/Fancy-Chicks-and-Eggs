About Fancy Chicks & Eggs

Your guide to backyard chicken keeping and sustainable living.

Our Mission

At Fancy Chicks & Eggs, our mission is to empower every individual with the knowledge and resources needed for successful and sustainable chicken keeping. We strive to foster a deep connection between our customers and their food sources, promoting healthier living and environmental responsibility.

Through comprehensive education and support, we aim to make chicken keeping accessible to all, regardless of their background or living situation. Join us in creating a future where backyard flocks contribute to sustainable food systems worldwide.
